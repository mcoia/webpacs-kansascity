Government Document (SuDoc & MoDoc) Number Search

Type as much or as little of the government document call number as you want. For example:

  • SuDoc Numbers
    • C 3.186/3-2:992
    • D 101.22:550-25/990
    • HE 20.3152:F 94/4
    • LC 33.10:
  • MoDoc Numbers
    • MO ECON To 12:9996-97
    • MO ED 4:sch 1/
    • MO NR.En 2:
    • MO PS.Hp 5:In 7/

TIP: Always type MO at the beginning of a MoDoc number.

SuDOC: number used for United States government documents;

MoDOC: call number used for Missouri State government documents.
